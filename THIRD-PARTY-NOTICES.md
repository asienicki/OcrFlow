## Dictionaries

This project includes word frequency lists used for spell correction.

### Polish and English word lists

Source: https://github.com/hermitdave/FrequencyWords  
Dataset: 2018 frequency lists  
License: https://github.com/hermitdave/FrequencyWords/blob/master/LICENSE

The dictionary files were generated by combining and trimming the original frequency lists
to approximately 50,000 entries per language.

The dictionary files are used as data-only resources and are not part of the application code.
